The Final Word · Closing Notes

Buon Appetito!

Your authentic Roman Carbonara is complete. The emulsion holds, the silk clings to the rigatoni, and the kitchen smells of pork and pepper. There is no cream. There is no regret.

i.Serve immediately in warm bowls — carbonara waits for no one, and the sauce sets as it cools.
ii.Top with reserved crispy guanciale, extra Pecorino, and a final crack of fresh black pepper.
iii.Pair with a cold, crisp Italian white — Frascati or Verdicchio cut through the richness beautifully.
