ii.The Second Step

Boiling Pasta

Molto al dente. Boil the spaghetti or rigatoni in heavily salted water — it should taste like the sea.

Starch concentration tip. Use slightly less water than the package suggests to concentrate the starches in the pot. Cloudy pasta water is golden, not wasteful.

Under-cook slightly. Pull the pasta out 2 minutes before package directions so it can finish in the rendering pan.
