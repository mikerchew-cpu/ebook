iv.The Final Step

The Final Emulsion

Four movements, one perfect sauce. Everything must move in six seconds.

i
+ 0 sec

Sauté Coat

Transfer al dente pasta directly into the skillet with warm guanciale fat; toss to coat evenly.

ii
+ 30 sec

Off-Heat Cool

Let the skillet sit off the burner for 30 seconds. This is the moment that prevents scrambled eggs.

iii
+ 60 sec

Pour Paste

Pour the yolk-cheese pastella over the pasta, then throw in the crispy guanciale pieces.

iv
+ 90 sec

Rapid Agitation

Add a ladle of pasta water. Toss aggressively to create a creamy, silk-like golden emulsion.
