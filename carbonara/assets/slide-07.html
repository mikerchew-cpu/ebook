i.The First Step

Slow Rendering

Guanciale releases its soul through patience. Low and slow is the only tempo that renders the fat silky while crisping the meat golden.

i.
Cut chunkilySlice guanciale into 1cm thick batons so they stay tender inside when crisp.
ii.
Start coldPlace pork into a cold pan with no extra oil; let the fat render out slowly.
iii.
Slow sautéCook on low-medium for 8–10 minutes until golden and crispy.
iv.
Retain the fatRemove the meat, but leave the rendered liquid fat base in the pan.
